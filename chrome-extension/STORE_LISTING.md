# Chrome Web Store Listing — 3BOX AI Auto Apply

## Name
3BOX AI — Auto Apply

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Auto-apply to jobs on LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, Glassdoor, Workday & iCIMS. See match scores and one-click apply with your 3BOX profile.

## Detailed Description
Stop spending hours filling out the same job application forms over and over.

3BOX AI Auto Apply detects when you're viewing a job posting and shows you an instant match score based on your profile. With one click, it auto-fills and submits applications using the information from your 3BOX AI account — saving you time and effort.

SUPPORTED JOB SITES:
• LinkedIn (Easy Apply)
• Indeed
• Naukri
• Glassdoor
• Workday portals
• iCIMS portals

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Sign in with your 3BOX AI account
2. Browse jobs on any supported site
3. See your match score on every job listing
4. Click "Auto Apply" to instantly submit your application

KEY FEATURES:
• Match Score — See how well you fit each job before applying
• One-Click Apply — Auto-fills name, email, phone, location, and more
• LinkedIn Easy Apply — Navigates multi-step modals automatically
• Dashboard Sync — Every application is tracked in your 3BOX AI dashboard
• Privacy First — Your data stays in your 3BOX account; nothing is shared with third parties

REQUIREMENTS:
• A 3BOX AI account (sign up free at https://3box.ai)
• An uploaded resume on your 3BOX profile

Need help? Visit https://3box.ai or contact support@3box.ai.

## Single Purpose Description
This extension auto-applies to job postings on supported career sites using the user's 3BOX AI profile data.

## Permission Justifications

### Host Permissions
- linkedin.com: Required to detect job listings and auto-fill Easy Apply forms on LinkedIn.
- indeed.com: Required to detect job listings and auto-fill application forms on Indeed.
- naukri.com: Required to detect job listings and auto-fill application forms on Naukri.
- glassdoor.com: Required to detect job listings and auto-fill application forms on Glassdoor.
- *.myworkdayjobs.com: Required to detect job listings and auto-fill application forms on Workday career portals.
- *.icims.com: Required to detect job listings and auto-fill application forms on iCIMS career portals.
- 3box.ai: Required to authenticate the user and sync application data with their 3BOX AI dashboard.

### Permissions
- storage: Store authentication token and user preferences locally.
- tabs: Detect when the user navigates to a supported job site.
- activeTab: Access the current tab to detect job postings and inject the match score badge.
- alarms: Schedule periodic token refresh checks.
